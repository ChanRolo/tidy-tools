Offline by design

File utilities that never upload your files.

Every popular "free online converter" works by sending your documents and photos to someone else's server. Tidy Tools work differently: everything runs locally in your browser — contracts, invoices, family photos stay on your machine. Even with Wi-Fi off.

TidyPDF Merge, split, rotate and reorder PDF pages. Turn images into PDFs. Drag pages between files like cards. Merge & split PDFs offline → TidyPic Convert HEIC, AVIF, WebP, PNG and JPG in bulk. iPhone photos that finally open everywhere — EXIF & GPS stripped automatically. Convert images offline → TidyDoc Turn Markdown from ChatGPT or Claude into polished Word, Excel and PDF files. The AI-to-office bridge. Convert AI output offline → AI Visibility WordPress plugin: publish llms.txt so AI assistants understand your site, and control which AI crawlers may use your content. Take charge of AI crawlers →
